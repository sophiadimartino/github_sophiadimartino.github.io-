Welcome to my Final Project Page!!

Hello!!Hello!! Kaylee and I made an educational app that teaches kids how to draw shapes. An educational app is useful for teachers
who are teaching their class how to draw certain shapes, letter, or numbers. We added in a circle, triangle, and square and three
colors for the child to choose from to learn how to draw! You start by pressing the start button, selecting the shape, then select
the color. Hope you enjoy!!
